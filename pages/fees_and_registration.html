Tennis Coaching Fees


In order to provide you with the flexibility of planning your own lessons anytime, BipBillyTennis charges each lesson individually.


Lesson Type Price
Individual Lessons 1 student to 1 coach $65/h
Pair Lessons 2 students to 1 coach $80/h (ie $40 each person)
Group Lessons 3 or 4 students to 1 coach $100/h
