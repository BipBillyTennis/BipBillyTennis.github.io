Kids Lesson

Discover your child's passion and cultivate sports value while having fun.

Our coaches provide timely feedback on your child's progression. Lesson focuses on building ball sense and techniques at the same time.

Adult Lesson

Whether you are learning a new sport or perfecting your strokes, our coaches got you covered.

Adult lesson focuses on techniques and building the muscle groups to improve in the sport. Our coaches are friendly and easy-going.




Individual Lesson

Have your lesson highly tailored to suit your progression. Whether you are just starting or trying to take your tennis to the next level, individual lessons will allow you to improve at the fastest pace possible.


Pair Lesson

Learn tennis with a friend or loved ones could strengthen your bond and allows you to have a sparring partner with the same skillset. Pair lessons have the best balance between having fun and learning.


Group Lesson (Max of 4 Pax)

The most affordable way to learn with us. Group sessions are kept at a maximum size of 4 peron to ensure gradual progression and allows our coaches to provide feedback to everyone in the group.
