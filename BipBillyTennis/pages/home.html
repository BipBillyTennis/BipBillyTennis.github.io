Our Mission

We aim deliver quality and affordable tennis lessons as we believe tennis is a sport for all ages and gender. Improvement, enjoyment and students' satisfaction is what we strive for in every lesson that you have with us.

Why BipBillyTennis?

We have a small team of experienced coach that provide targetted and specialized feedback to you. Not all experienced players can be good coaches, the reason being many of the actions and strokes experienced players perform becomes instinctive. Our coaches are capable of providing clear instructions on the execution of every stroke.
